What Sticks Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 29 January 2024
Welcome to What Sticks! We are committed to pioneering modernized wellness solutions while rigorously protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information through our What Sticks Platform (the "Platform").

1. Information Collection

When you use our Platform, we collect the following types of information:
  • Personal Information: We collect personal information that you provide to us, such as your email address, when you register for an account.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with our Platform, like the features you use and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
  • Wellness Information: We collect information approved by the user such as steps counts, heart rates, workout durations, etc.,

2. Use of Information

We use your information for the following purposes:
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Platform and services.
  • To personalize your experience.
  • For benchmarking purposes, where your data is aggregated with other users' data to help you understand your wellness journey in comparison to broader trends.
  • For research and development purposes, always in aggregate and anonymized form.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Aggregate Information: We may share aggregated and anonymized information with users or research teams interested in wellness trends. This data will never contain your email or personally identifiable information.
  • Evaluation of Data Requests: If there are requests for the anonymous, aggregated data, the What Sticks team will evaluate these requests and may share the data without user emails or any personally identifiable information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe that the disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored on What Sticks Servers. We employ a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at nrodrig1@gmail.com.
